Overview

TinyPalette is a coloring and educational application created for children.

We are committed to protecting the privacy of children, parents and guardians by collecting as little information as possible.

Privacy First TinyPalette does not require an account, registration or login to use the application.

Information We Collect

TinyPalette itself does not ask users to provide personal information.

Data Collected
Name No
Email Address No
Phone Number No
Account No
Cloud Storage No

Advertising

TinyPalette displays advertisements using Google AdMob to help keep the application free to use.

Important Advertisements are provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain information such as device identifiers, diagnostics and advertising information in accordance with Google's own privacy policies.

Internet Connection

TinyPalette only requires an internet connection to request and display advertisements. Coloring, drawing and learning features continue to function without an internet connection.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Google Play Services

Drawings & Device Storage

Artwork created using TinyPalette is stored locally on your device.

TinyPalette does not upload drawings to our servers.

If you choose to save an artwork to your gallery or share it using another application, that action is initiated and controlled by you.

Your Artwork Belongs To You TinyPalette does not maintain cloud backups, online accounts or remote storage for your drawings.

Children's Privacy

TinyPalette is intended for use by children under the supervision of parents or guardians.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to review the application with their children and supervise internet usage when advertisements are displayed.

Google Play Families Policy

TinyPalette has been designed with the Google Play Families requirements in mind and aims to provide age-appropriate content for young users.

Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect improvements to the application, legal requirements or service changes.

Any updates will be published on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or TinyPalette, please contact us.
